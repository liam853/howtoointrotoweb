Matcha Brownies

By Marc Matusumoto

Link here

These Matcha Brownies will give you a treat that is decadent and tasteful. The matcha from the brownies give a nice bitterness, while the white chocolate mixed into the batter gives a sweet, sticky flavor

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Matcha Powder
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • White Chocolate
  • Cultured Unsalted Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cane Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

    • Step 1

    1. Prepare a 8x8 baking sheet with parchment paper
    2. Preheat a oven to 350 degrees

    Step 2

    Sift flour, matcha, baking powder, salt till combined

    Step 3

  • add white chocolate and butter into a bowl
    1. microwave at 300 watts for 1 & 1/2 minuets
    2. stir chocolate until there are no lumps
    3. beat eggs into chocolate until uniform
    4. add sugar and vanilla into this mix until smooth

    Step 4

    Pour wet ingridents into dry and mix until smooth

    Step 5

    Dump batter into baking tray and bake for 18-20 minuets

    Step 6

    Remove brownies from baking tray using parchment paper to cool
